Scholar Profile

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Iowa State University investigating how song lyrics and AI-generated music can be used to teach connected speech and pronunciation.

My work integrates corpus linguistics, generative AI, acoustic analysis, and language assessment to explore linguistic variation across human- and AI-generated texts.

Primary Research

Dissertation and corpus projects focused on pronunciation, connected speech, and AI vs. human-authored song lyrics.

Dissertation · In Progress
